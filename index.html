The idea

Built around one simple truth

Most task apps make you manage your tasks. CarryOver just keeps them with you. Close your laptop tonight — they'll be waiting tomorrow. No setup, no reminders, no complexity. Just your list.

Features

Automatic rollover

Unfinished tasks carry forward to today automatically. Nothing slips through the cracks, ever.

Keyboard first

Add, navigate, complete, and delete tasks entirely from the keyboard. Your hands never leave home row.

Status bar native

Lives in your menu bar, completely out of your way. One click and it's there. Close it and it's gone.

Day history

Browse past days to see what you completed. Your daily record, always there when you need it.

Global hotkey

Open CarryOver from anywhere with a customisable hotkey. Add a task in seconds, without breaking your flow.

Undo everything

Deleted a task by mistake? Toggled it wrong? Undo your last action instantly with ⌘Z.

CarryOver is designed to be used without touching the mouse.
